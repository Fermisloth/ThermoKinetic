THE BIO-DIGITAL TWIN PIPELINE

From IoT Telemetry to Dynamic Expiry in Five Steps

See how ThermoKinetic transforms raw sensor data into actionable cold-chain intelligence.

1

Ingest

IoT telemetry streams — temperature, humidity, vibration, GPS — are continuously ingested from shipment sensors via MQTT and REST endpoints at sub-second intervals.

2

Model

AKM applies multi-order Arrhenius kinetic equations to the real-time thermal profile, selecting the best-fit degradation model via BIC optimization across isothermal and fluctuating conditions.

3

Twin

A Bio-Digital Twin of each product unit continuously updates its degradation state — tracking antigenicity, potency, and structural integrity in real time against the kinetic model.

4

Score

The Dynamic Expiry QR code is recalculated every cycle — encoding remaining potency, predicted shelf life, and compliance status into a scannable mobile-verifiable credential.

5

Act

Compliance officers receive real-time alerts. Routes are automatically adjusted. Shipments are cleared, rerouted, or flagged — all before a single vial degrades below threshold.
