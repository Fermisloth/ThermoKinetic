REGULATORY ALIGNMENT

Built for the Regulators You Answer To

ThermoKinetic's Stability-as-a-Service model is designed from the ground up to satisfy global cold-chain compliance requirements.

WHO PQS Compliance

ThermoKinetic satisfies WHO Performance, Quality, and Safety prequalification requirements for temperature monitoring equipment and cold-chain management systems. Our platform enables continuous documentation of storage and transport conditions.

WHO/PQS/E003/TR07.2

DSCSA & XATP Serialization

Full compliance with the Drug Supply Chain Security Act through XATP blockchain-backed serialization. Every unit is tracked from manufacturer to dispenser with immutable chain-of-custody records and verifiable credentials.

21 CFR Part 211.150

WHO IMD-PQS 2025–2030

Aligned with the WHO Immunization and Devices Prequalification Strategy 2025-2030. ThermoKinetic supports the transition from static monitoring to predictive intelligence for national immunization supply chains.

IMD/PQS/2025-2030/Rev.1

ThermoKinetic's StaaS model enables Prequalification Holders to document compliance and prove to regulators that required conditions were maintained throughout the entire supply chain — from manufacturing to last-mile delivery.
